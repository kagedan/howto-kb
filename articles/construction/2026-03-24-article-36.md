“Verification matters most where one mistake is catastrophic.”

@CarinaLHong on where verified AI is gaining traction:

“Code verification isn’t just a math problem. It shows up in chip design, large enterprise software, civil engineering, cryptography, and legacy system migrations.”

“We’re seeing customers come to us saying: this wasn’t possible before, but with formal math, now it is.”

“The biggest pull is in mission-critical domains. Healthcare. Insurance. Anything where human lives or massive liability are at stake.”

“There’s value in catching catastrophic edge cases. But there’s also value in being able to trust that the edge cases are covered.”

“Right now, that lack of trust is what blocks generative AI from being deployed at scale.”
