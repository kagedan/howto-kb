Smart Cities & Infrastructure
•Autonomous Traffic Management™: AI systems that use real-time data from sensors, cameras, and vehicles to dynamically control traffic lights, reroute flows, and coordinate autonomous vehicles, reducing congestion and accidents.
•AI Road Maintenance Analytics™: Machine learning models analyze pavement conditions, weather, and usage data to predict deterioration and prioritize repairs before problems worsen.
•Smart Street Lighting™: Adaptive lighting networks that dim or brighten based on detected traffic, pedestrians, or ambient light, cutting energy use while improving visibility and safety.
•Intelligent Waste Collection™: Sensor-equipped bins and AI route optimization direct collection vehicles only when needed, lowering fuel consumption and operational costs.
•Flood Prediction Network™: Integrated IoT sensors and predictive AI models forecast flooding risks by combining rainfall, river levels, and topography data for early alerts.
•Smart Bridge Monitoring™: Continuous sensor data fed into AI algorithms detect structural stress, cracks, or corrosion in real time for preventive maintenance.
•Digital Twin City Platform™: A virtual 3D replica of a city that simulates infrastructure changes, traffic, or disasters to support better urban planning and decision-making.
•AI Construction Site Monitoring™: Computer vision and AI track worker safety, equipment usage, and project progress on construction sites to reduce delays and hazards.
•Autonomous Public Transport Hub™: Self-managing transit stations that handle scheduling, passenger flow, ticketing, and transfers with minimal human oversight.
•Smart Utility Grid™: Intelligent power, water, and gas networks that balance supply/demand, detect outages or leaks, and integrate renewable sources automatically.
