My first article, NFA, an explanation from my understanding of this company. I will explain from the (literal) ground up.

Some basic info before we start for the newcomers. $VIVO is in the middle of a transition to a bricks and mortar data center builder with the intention of long term leasing their projects. ## **1. The New Land Acquisition Team (FCDC)**

Vivo has been buying land from FCDC/OGDC, they have LOIs for the full portfolio.

The FCDC business model is buying Brownfield/Greenfield land, demolishing old buildings + connecting to the national grid, then flipping that connected land to bigger players for profit.

The owners of FCDC  have now joined the $VIVO  team. They’ve taken payment in the form of $13M up front, with the rest in stock at conversion prices of $15 a share. Important to note this is also due to convert only once the land gets powered on in Q1 2027. 

$VIVO is only trading at $5.45 right now. The $15 conversion price is a bold bet from the FCDC/OGDC team. They understand the worth of the Finland land next year, and the premium this places on $VIVO. 

So Vivo power have absorbed a talented team of land buyers/connectors with a pristine track record. However this time they won’t be flipping. $VIVO is leveraging their skills into future pipeline acquisition.
