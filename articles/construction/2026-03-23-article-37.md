📌Mons finally has its station! https://t.co/N33kGJC8Jz

After nearly two decades of construction, @S_Calatrava’s Gare de Mons has opened as a sculptural gateway for the Belgian city, already hailed as one of the most beautiful train stations in the world. 

🛠️ Learn how to merge AI with animation and parametric workflows in architecture: https://t.co/kwXNnZfNqH

The design unfolds as a sweeping concrete and steel structure, where Calatrava’s signature ribs stretch into a vast, light-filled canopy that connects platforms, public spaces, and the city beyond.

The station bridges old and new Mons with a single architectural gesture that feels both infrastructural and almost organismal, transforming mobility into an experience of movement, shadow, and flow. It stands as a reminder that transit hubs can be civic landmarks, not just corridors of transit.
