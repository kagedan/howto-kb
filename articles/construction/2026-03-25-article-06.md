Anthropic’s labour study flags highest AI exposure in
• Computer & math
• Business & finance
• Management
• Legal
• Office & administrative roles.

In contrast, manual and physical jobs ( construction, agriculture, repair, personal services) remain relatively insulated.

The more digitised your job, the higher the AI disruption risk and many of these sectors( IT, BPO, finance and back-office services ) employ millions in India.
