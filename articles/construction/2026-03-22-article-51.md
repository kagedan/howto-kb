![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HCH-iDyacAA6Tdz.jpg)

he didn't do it because the company is bleeding out. gross profit grew 24% year over year to $2.87 billion. cash app gross profit surged 33%. earnings hit estimates. the business is genuinely healthy by every traditional metric you could point to.

he did it because AI made nearly half the company unnecessary while the numbers kept climbing.

and if you think this is a block story, you're not paying attention. this is YOUR story. it's just that nobody has told you yet.
