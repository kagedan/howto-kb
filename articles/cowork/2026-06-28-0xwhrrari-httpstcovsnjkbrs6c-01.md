## What Claude Cowork Actually Is

Most people still think of Claude as a chatbot.

You open a chat.

You type a prompt.

Claude replies.

You copy the answer somewhere else.

That is useful.

But it is still manual.

Claude Cowork is different.

It runs inside the desktop app and works with files on your actual computer.

It can:

- **Read documents**
- **Create folders**
- **Rename files**
- **Write reports**
- **Build spreadsheets**
- **Draft presentations**
- **Organize messy folders**
- **Work through multi-step tasks**
- **Ask clarifying questions before it starts**
The important part:

Cowork does not just tell you what to do.

It does the work and puts the output where it belongs.

That is why it matters for a one-person business.

You do not need more ideas.

You need production capacity.

## The Mindset Shift

Most people use AI like this:

```plaintext
Ask question
↓
Get answer
↓
Copy answer
↓
Paste into another tool
↓
Format manually
↓
Save manually
```

That is still you doing the workflow.

Cowork should be used like this:

```plaintext
Define outcome
↓
Give access to the right folder
↓
Let Cowork inspect the files
↓
Approve the plan
↓
Cowork creates the deliverable
↓
