Fable 5 just dropped and you only have until June 22 to really make use of it before you join the permanent AI underclass.

Side note: if you planned on using Mythos for any kind of white hat cybersecurity work, kiss that goodbye. It won't do it.

BUT, here are five permanent things to run now that will outlive Fable and optimize your Opus workflow even more.

1. A full setup audit. Point it at every CLAUDE.md, every memory file, your Obsidian vault if you have one. Consolidate, kill the bloat, let it restructure whatever slows the system down. Think claude doctor for your entire setup. 

2. A skill audit. Every skill you've built. It finds issues fixes them, and flags optimizations the other models never caught. 

3. A rules rewrite for the weaker model. Your rules.md works today because a smart model fills the gaps with judgment. Have Fable stress-test them for ambiguity and rewrite them explicit enough that Opus 4.8 can follow without judgment calls.

4. Session-log mining. Have it read every session log you have. Based on that, update your memory, Obsidian, turn corrections into rules or hooks.. Everything you do daily becomes an SOP. 

5. Flip your orchestration. Until now Opus orchestrated Sonnet and Haiku subagents. Now Fable orchestrates Opus: writes its prompts, runs Opus agents for research, and builds the prompt and workflow library Opus will execute after it's gone.

12 days left.
