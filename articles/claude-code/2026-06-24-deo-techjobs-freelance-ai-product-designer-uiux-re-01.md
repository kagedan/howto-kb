🌍 Remote
💼 Freelance

Are you a Product Designer who uses AI as more than just a buzzword?

🎨 Designing end-to-end UX and UI for complex digital products

🤝 Collaborating with product managers and engineers to align design with business goals
🧩 Building, maintaining, and evolving design systems
🧪 Conducting user testing and improving experiences based on real feedback
📈 Using data and user behavior insights to drive design decisions
💡 Demonstrating how AI enhances both product experiences and design workflows

What they’re looking for:

✅ 5–10 years of experience designing complex B2B and B2C products
✅ Hands-on experience using AI tools in production design workflows
✅ Experience integrating Figma MCP with Cursor, Claude Code, or similar AI-assisted workflows
✅ Strong product thinking and problem-solving skills
✅ Proven experience conducting and analyzing user research
✅ Advanced Figma skills, including scalable design systems
✅ Strong portfolio showcasing AI-driven design processes and high-quality UI work

This role is ideal for designers who are already embracing AI as part of their daily workflow and want to help shape the future of product design.
