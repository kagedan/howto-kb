Anthropic's growth marketing team has been one non-technical operator running every channel with Claude for the past ten months.

Paid search. Paid social. App stores. Email. SEO. All of it. https://t.co/03izSQjrFd

The workflow is almost funny. Export ads and performance data into a CSV. Hand it to Claude Code. Let it find the weak links. Spin up new headlines by the hundreds. A Figma plugin turns winners into real creative.

A decade ago this job took ten people, three agencies, and a project manager keeping briefs on time.

Founders still want a VP of marketing, head of paid, head of SEO, and head of lifecycle. For a seed stage company.

Wrong question. The right one: do you have one operator who knows the craft cold and will live inside the tools?

If yes, you need a few of them and a lot of compute. Not a full department.

The old org chart used to be a moat. It's starting to feel like a tax.

What's the leanest marketing team you've seen run by a real operator?
