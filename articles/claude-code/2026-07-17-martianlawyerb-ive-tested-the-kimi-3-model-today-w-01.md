I've tested the Kimi 3 model today with 20$ and tbh its quite underwhelming. Working with Deepseek V4 Pro was way more effective for my workflow and orders of magnitude cheaper.

I blew through nearly 20$ (and got 5$ for free) in a couple of hours of API usage with Claude Code and it is a pain to make it do what i want. Not sure if its me or my workload but I am disappointed.

That said I ideologically like that they are promising to release the weights and push the open weights frontier forward. But open weights don't really make much of a difference to me if the model is so big I can't run it on consumer hardware anyways. It might make it cheaper if providers compete on pricing for inference though. 

We will see.
