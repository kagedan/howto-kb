This isn’t just “better notes.” It’s a **living AI co-founder** that never forgets and works while you sleep.

## Why scattered notes and chat tabs fail hard in 2026

Most people still copy-paste into a new chat and close the tab. The result?

- 60-70% of context disappears between sessions
- Ideas never connect
- You re-solve the same problems
- Your content and projects stay average because there’s no deep personal history fueling them
In 2026, the winners aren’t the ones with more tools. They’re the ones with **persistent memory + an AI that actively maintains it**.
