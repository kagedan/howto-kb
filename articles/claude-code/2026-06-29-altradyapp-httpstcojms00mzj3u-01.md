## How to connect Altrady to Claude with MCP and actually trade by talking to it

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HL-08MdbIAABtIO.jpg)

What if you could run your entire crypto trading setup just by talking to it? No menus, no twelve open tabs. You type "show me what's moving today," and your trading platform actually answers.

That's exactly what we're setting up here: turning Claude into a real AI trading assistant by connecting it to Altrady through something called MCP. By the end you'll be able to scan markets, analyze charts, and prepare trades in plain English. And don't worry — you still approve every single order. The AI never moves your money on its own. If you're brand new to this, you're in exactly the right place.

## WHAT YOU NEED BEFORE YOU START

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HL-1Hapa0AAEJ4O.jpg)

Three things, and that's it:

1. The Altrady desktop app. This is a crypto trading platform that connects to your exchange accounts so you can trade and track everything from one place. It runs on Windows and Mac, and there's a free trial so you can follow along without paying anything.
1. The Claude desktop app from Anthropic. Claude is the AI we'll actually be talking to, it reads your charts, scans the markets, and preps your trades. Make sure it's the actual desktop application, not just the website in your browser, because the MCP connection needs the desktop app.
1. A crypto exchange account, Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, KuCoin and more. Altrady already supports over 15 of them.
## WHAT IS MCP?

![](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/HL-1VGkbYAA6w8O.jpg)

You'll hear the term a lot. MCP stands for Model Context Protocol. Forget the technical name, here's the simple version: MCP is a bridge that lets an AI assistant talk to an app on your computer.
