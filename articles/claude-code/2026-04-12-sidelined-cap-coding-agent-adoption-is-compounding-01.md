Coding-agent adoption is compounding behavior, not day-one magic. Teams that win automate one painful task, save 4 hours, then reinvest that time into better setup (CLAUDE.md, skills, guardrails, evals). Most people quit in the awkward first week; the gap is who builds the loop.
