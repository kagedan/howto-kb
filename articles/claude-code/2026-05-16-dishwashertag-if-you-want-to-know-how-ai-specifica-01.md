If you want to know how AI, specifically Agents &amp; Automation, can help you while working, building, or creating, read on.

TL;DR: AI is a tool that allows you to optimize any workflow. And there is no ceiling.

1/

AI may not generate the most maintainable codebase.

AI may not solve the hardest technical problems.

AI may not write the next great novel.

AI may not understand the contemporary Zeitgeist.

2/

But here is what AI definitely will do:

&gt; AI will help speed up any workflow.

&gt; AI will help you make better decisions by gathering, curating, and presenting information right when you need it.

3/

&gt; AI will help you stay in flow for longer, which means you will be more productive and more creative.

Knowledge work at the speed of thought is the promise of AI.

It is going to be a long journey to get there.

The way I think about it starts here:

4/

When you are working,
building,
or creating,
then what you are really doing is playing a game.

And in every game, there is a goal.

And if work is a game, then at any given moment, there is a next optimal play. Making that play gets you one step closer to your goal.

5/

To figure out what that next best play is, you need two things: Taste and Information.

In technical endeavors, taste is the ability to solve a problem based on the patterns you have internalized from solving problems in the past.

6/

In artistic endeavors, taste is the ability to create something that evokes an uplifting emotion in as many people as possible, based on past experiences that triggered certain emotions in you or in the people around you.

7/

Taste is the intersection of experience and creativity.

Taste cannot be taught. 

Taste is cultivated by being intrinsically motivated to do *a thing* and then actually doing that thing. 

(Which is why agency is also a nice trait to have)

8/
