Got a killer workflow using Claude Code, skills, or plugins? Now you can share it with the world! 

Build with Claude(https://t.co/Bu0znZDhkd) just launched Stories, a dedicated space to showcase how you actually use skills, plugins, and MCP servers to solve real problems.

The best part? You do not even have to be the creator. Whether you built a tool yourself or just mastered someone else's to supercharge your daily workflow, you can contribute.

Why share your story?
- Showcase real solutions: Help others understand exactly what a skill solves and how to use it.

- Share your workflows: Put your unique setups, prompts, and automation loops on display.

- Get featured: Your post lands right on the homepage strip for the community to see.

How it works?
It is a simple, developer friendly open source flow:

Fork the repo:
- Drop a folder with your Markdown file in stories/<your-slug>/ 📁

- Open a PR. Once merged, it goes live in minutes! ⚡

- Check out the quick walkthrough to ship your first post: 👉 https://t.co/yRNMS3ywVT
