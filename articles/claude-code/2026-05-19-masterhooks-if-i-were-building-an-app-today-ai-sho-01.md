If I were building an app today, AI should be in every part of your workflow.

•⁠  ⁠Cursor or Bolt to get the MVP moving fast.
•⁠  ⁠⁠Claude Code or Codex for backend, APIs, auth, database.
•⁠  ⁠⁠Claude Design or v0 to explore UI directions before hiring a designer.
•⁠  ⁠⁠ChatGPT or Claude for App Store copy, positioning, and keywords.
•⁠  ⁠⁠Runway or Kling to create early creative concepts for marketing.
•⁠  ⁠⁠NotebookLM or Claude as a strategy sparring partner to pressure-test the product vision.

AI won’t replace founder judgment.
but it will make you move faster.
