## Where this story begins

A little over a year ago, I gave my friend @LGMartelotto  the first live demonstration of my Biomedical Operating System — an agentic framework built on Skills + MCP (Model Context Protocol) + Agentic AI that performs end-to-end computational biology tasks. Work that takes a trained specialist weeks to months was completed, in front of him, in minutes.

Since then, the ground has shifted dramatically under all of us working at the intersection of AI and biology. In subsequent conversations with the @AnthropicAI  @claudeai  team and the @OpenAI  @Codex team, both indicated interest in integrating my open framework:

**LLMs — Universal Life-Science and Clinical Skills: **[github.com/mdbabumiamssm/LLMs-Universal-Life-Science-and-Clinical-Skills-](http://github.com/mdbabumiamssm/LLMs-Universal-Life-Science-and-Clinical-Skills-)

But excitement is one thing! The field is full of impressive demos that quietly collapse the moment you ask for rigor. So I held my own system to the hardest standard I know and was suggested by [Luciano Martelotto](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAUwpL0B1x1ya4cyOPRZb-rTRx6YL0bZHz0?miniProfileUrn=urn%3Ali%3Afs_miniProfile%3AACoAAAUwpL0B1x1ya4cyOPRZb-rTRx6YL0bZHz0) !
