Forget "AI trading bot."

Spectre is trying to build the Bloomberg Terminal for crypto.

One place that combines:

• On-chain data
• Whale tracking
• Macro
• Social sentiment
• Derivatives
• AI reasoning

Into a single interface. https://t.co/LKQV7xZ6FG

2/ Why People Bought The Story

Crypto research is fragmented. One tab for TradingView.

Another for X. Another for on-chain. Another for macro.

Spectre's pitch was simple:

Why not let AI connect everything for you?

That idea attracted a lot of attention during the AI boom.

5/ The Big Bet

The thesis isn't another chatbot.

It's becoming the operating system for market research.

If traders spend hours switching between dashboards today...

Spectre wants to become the single interface they open first.

That's an ambitious goal.

The problem isn't that the product doesn't exist.

Everyone wants to own AI-assisted research.

You're competing against:
