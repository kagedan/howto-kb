Anthropic Claude クリエイティブツール連携（9コネクター） Anthropic が Adobe Creative Cloud、Blender、Autodesk Fusion、Ableton Live など9つのクリエイティブツール向けコネクターをリリース。
MCP ベースで Claude がこれらのアプリを直接操作できるようになり、AI × クリエイティブワークフローが大きく前進した。


--- 引用元 @WesRoth ---
Anthropic released nine new Claude connectors designed for creative professionals. 

This rollout integrates Claude directly into industry-standard software, including Adobe Creative Cloud, Blender, Autodesk Fusion, Affinity by Canva, Ableton Live, Splice, SketchUp, and Resolume.  

Powered by the open Model Context Protocol (MCP), Claude can now natively execute commands and modify files directly within these applications. 

For example, a 3D artist can ask Claude to debug an entire Blender scene or batch-apply changes to objects, while a designer can use natural language to create and modify 3D models in Autodesk Fusion or automate repetitive layer adjustments in Affinity.
