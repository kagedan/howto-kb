## First, why bother with volume at all

If your plan is one polished post a week, you don't need this stack. But the data says that plan is losing.

The clearest numbers come from paid advertising, because paid is where every creative gets measured to the dollar. Brands testing 20+ new creatives a month see roughly 65% higher ROAS than brands testing fewer than 10. Creative fatigue now sets in within 2 to 4 weeks. Teams that keep refreshing see acquisition costs drop 20 to 35%.

The same physics govern organic feeds. More concepts, fresher, less polished. That's what gets distribution on IG and TikTok right now.

And here's the practical problem: text is no longer the expensive part. Any decent model writes 20 caption variants in seconds. The expensive part is visuals. Twenty pieces of on-brand imagery and video normally means a designer, an editor, and a week of revisions.
